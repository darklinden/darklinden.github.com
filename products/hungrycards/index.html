HungryCards

Description

    This is a game with number and puzzle.

    Card is the protagonist of the game with 4 sides of numbers.

    One card can "eat" another if the number on the adjoining side is stronger than the other one.

    After a complete "meal", the number on the special side will increase.

    The card will be "too full" if one of the numbers is greater than 9, in this case, the card cannot be moved.

    You may move one card to eat a pile of card as a combo to gain much more scores and move points.

    One move cost one move point, and there's no move point to use, the game is over.

    Thanks! Hope you enjoy the game.
