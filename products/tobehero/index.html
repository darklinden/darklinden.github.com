ToBeHero

Description

    - Duel with the Monster on the ice ground.

    - You can never beat the Monster at frontal attack. Play some tricks to attack from its back.

    - Now you have 6 points of life, or that, the game will be over if you've been hit 6 times.

    - Hit the Monster to score points.

    - Change control settings in the pause page, you can select the Accelerometer or the virtual Joystick.
